Smart Nesting Step Stool with Folding Mechanism

This is a space-saving, 3-in-1 nesting step stool featuring a unique pivoting mechanism that allows all three sections to fold cleanly into a single compact unit. Perfect for small spaces, workshops, or kitchens!
